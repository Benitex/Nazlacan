# Nazlacan

The Suns are taking over Nazlacan. You need to choose how much of yourself you're willing to sacrifice to them to survive.

Nazlacan is a (early in development) 3D survivors-like / roguelike game.
You can choose between 4 Suns with a different range of skills and equipment to increase your power, but the stronger in power you get,
the greater the influence of that Sun in the world, and the stronger the enemies become.
Many of the themes and the gameplay are inspired by the Mexica (Aztec) culture and beliefs.
Made with Unreal Engine, C++, and Blueprints.
